# 1. Light-mode contrast

- **[Severity: Low] The base muted-text tokens already pass AA and should not be darkened globally.** In light mode, `--text-secondary: #52525b` is approximately **7.16:1 on `--bg-page: #f6f6f8`** and **7.73:1 on `--bg-surface: #fff`**; `--text-tertiary: #6b6b76` is approximately **4.88:1** and **5.26:1**, respectively (`nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:91`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:92`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:100`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:101`). **Fix:** Keep both token values; remove local opacity before changing either shared token.
- **[Severity: High] Small accent-colored text fails on the page background and on accent-tinted pills.** `--accent: #1f6feb` is approximately **4.29:1 on `#f6f6f8`**, and only about **3.88:1** when its 8% `--accent-dim` tint is composited over the page; affected normal-size text includes the project hero metadata and flow pills (`nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:103`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:104`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/projects/[slug]/page.tsx:517`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/projects/[slug]/page.tsx:727`). **Fix:** Nudge the light `--accent` to roughly `#1b64d8` (about **5.04:1** on the page and **4.52:1** on its matching 8% tint).
- **[Severity: High] Dark primary text on solid accent controls is below AA.** `--text-primary: #14141a` on `--accent: #1f6feb` is approximately **3.96:1**, affecting selected work filters and solid “Live Demo” links (`nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:99`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:559`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/components/WorkGrid.tsx:132`). **Fix:** Add/use a light-mode `--text-on-accent: #fff` for solid accent fills (approximately **4.63:1** now and **5.44:1** with `#1b64d8`) instead of `--text-primary`.
- **[Severity: High] The light success token fails for normal text.** `--color-success: #059669` is approximately **3.49:1 on the page**, **3.77:1 on white**, and **3.64:1 on the effective glass surface**; it is used for 12px outcome labels (`nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:114`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/projects/[slug]/page.tsx:848`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/components/mdx/MetricCard.tsx:30`). - **[Severity: Medium] Opacity drops an otherwise-safe secondary label below AA.** The 80%-opaque `#52525b` row label composites to about `#73737a`, approximately **4.36:1 on `#f6f6f8`**, below 4.5:1 for its 14px text (`nextjs-portfolio/src/components/FeaturedProject.tsx:32`). **Fix:** Remove `opacity-80` and rely on `--text-secondary` directly.
- **[Severity: Medium] Light borders are decorative-strength, not control-boundary strength.** On `#f6f6f8`, `--border-subtle` is approximately **1.11:1**, `--border-muted`/`--glass-border` **1.23:1**, `--border-active` **1.48:1**, and `--glass-border-bright` **1.41:1**, all below 3:1 when a boundary is needed to identify a control (`nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:95`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:96`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:97`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:127`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:128`). - **[Severity: Low] Glass text is already legible; its edge, not its text, is the risk.** `--glass-bg` over the page is approximately `#fbfbfc`; secondary and tertiary text remain about **7.47:1** and **5.09:1** on it, and the light nav pill resolves similarly (`nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:117`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:125`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:362`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:378`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/globals.css:413`). **Fix:** Do not darken glass backgrounds or text; strengthen only meaningful control boundaries as above.

# 2. Accessibility

- **[Severity: Medium] Homepage project/article headings do not consistently sit below their section heading.** “Selected Work” is an `h2`, but each featured project also renders as `h2`, while “Engineering Philosophy & Tooling” jumps from the About `h2` to `h4` (`nextjs-portfolio/src/app/home-content.tsx:210`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/components/FeaturedProject.tsx:21`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/home-content.tsx:501`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/home-content.tsx:513`). **Fix:** Let `FeaturedProject` accept a heading level and render `h3` on the homepage, and change the About `h4` to `h3`.
- **[Severity: Medium] The work grid can jump directly from its `h1` to compact-project `h3`s.** Categories with no featured item expose this path immediately (`nextjs-portfolio/src/components/WorkGrid.tsx:107`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/components/WorkGrid.tsx:156`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/components/CompactProject.tsx:39`). **Fix:** Add an `h2` for the compact-results tier (visually hidden if necessary), or make the compact heading level context-aware.
- **[Severity: Medium] The supplemental case-study section is introduced by an `h3` alongside page-level `h2` sections.** The wrapper creates a new sibling `<section>`, while the actual Luxe Spa narrative starts at level 3 (`nextjs-portfolio/src/app/projects/[slug]/page.tsx:471`, `nextjs-portfolio/content/case-studies/luxe-spa.mdx:11`). **Fix:** Start each narrative with an `h2` whose `id` labels the wrapper section.
- **[Severity: Medium] Several interactive targets are below the requested 44px minimum.** Examples include 36px homepage social icons and shorter marketplace links, 36px desktop TOC links, compact testimonial-source links, inline resume contact/profile links, and blog article links without a minimum height (`nextjs-portfolio/src/app/home-content.tsx:439`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/home-content.tsx:456`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/components/ProjectToc.tsx:106`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/components/TestimonialSpotlight.tsx:98`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/resume/page.tsx:327`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/blog/page.tsx:97`). **Fix:** Give these anchors/buttons `min-h-11` and, for icon-only controls, `min-w-11`, using padding without changing their visual hierarchy.
- **[Severity: Medium] Work filters are exposed as a navigation landmark even though they change client-side results.** The `<nav>` contains buttons rather than links and does not navigate (`nextjs-portfolio/src/components/WorkGrid.tsx:118`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/components/WorkGrid.tsx:125`). **Fix:** Replace it with a labelled `<fieldset>`/`<legend>` or `<div role="group" aria-label="Work category filters">` while retaining `aria-pressed`.

- **[Severity: Medium] The theme switcher uses ARIA radio semantics without radio keyboard behavior.** All three custom radios remain separate Tab stops and no arrow-key/roving-tabindex behavior is implemented (`nextjs-portfolio/src/components/theme/theme-switcher.tsx:22`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/components/theme/theme-switcher.tsx:29`). **Fix:** Either use native radio inputs in a labelled fieldset or simplify to buttons with `aria-pressed` so the keyboard contract matches the implementation.
- **[Severity: Medium] The resume's star-only rating has no accessible value.** Five icons visually carry the rating, but their container has no label and the icons are not hidden (`nextjs-portfolio/src/app/resume/page.tsx:481`, `nextjs-portfolio/src/app/resume/page.tsx:483`). **Fix:** Add `aria-label={`${featuredReview.rating} out of 5 stars`}` to the container and `aria-hidden="true"` to each star, matching `TestimonialSpotlight`.
