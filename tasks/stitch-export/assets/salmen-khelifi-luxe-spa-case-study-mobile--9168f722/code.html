Overview

What it is

A comprehensive multi-service white-label booking, CRM, and shop management SaaS platform. The system is designed to allow service-oriented businesses to launch custom-branded online booking experiences.

The problem it solves

Service businesses face a trade-off: use expensive SaaS platforms that charge high transaction fees, or build custom software that is slow and costly to maintain. This provides localized agencies an offering without rebuilding engines.

Who it's for

Independent boutique operators, local clinic chains, and digital agencies looking to offer localized booking platforms as a service.
